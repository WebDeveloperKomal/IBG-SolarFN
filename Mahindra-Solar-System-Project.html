Mahindra Solar Project:

The Mahindra Solar System Project exemplifies the Mahindra Group's foray into solar energy solutions. This initiative encompasses the installation and operation of solar power systems, furthering Mahindra's commitment to sustainability and green energy. The project exemplifies how major corporations like Mahindra are actively participating in the transition towards cleaner and more efficient energy sources.

Our scope of works includes:

  • Review of key technologies (PV modules, inverters, transformer)
  • Review of energy yield assessment
  • Historical performance data
  • Technical inputs to the financial model.
