Overview

At IBG Solar, we offer a diverse range of wind turbines, encompassing both vertical and horizontal axis designs. Our wind turbines cater to various applications, from powering auxiliary systems on boats and caravans to providing electricity for traffic warning signs. For larger-scale projects, our turbines can make significant contributions to residential power supplies while allowing customers to sell excess energy back to the utility grid. These turbines, when deployed in arrays known as wind farms, play a pivotal role in bolstering intermittent renewable energy sources, aligning with our commitment to reducing reliance on fossil fuels.

It's worth noting that a comprehensive assessment conducted in 2009 highlighted wind energy's exceptional environmental and social benefits. Wind power was found to exhibit the 'lowest relative greenhouse gas emissions, the least water consumption demands, and the most favorable social impacts' when compared to other energy sources, including photovoltaic, hydro, geothermal, coal, and gas. At IBG Solar, we take pride in offering sustainable solutions that prioritize both environmental responsibility and social well-being.
