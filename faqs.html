Frequently Asked Questions

How does solar energy work?

Solar energy is a revolutionary technology that harnesses the power of the sun to create clean and renewable electricity. By utilizing solar panels made up of photovoltaic cells, sunlight is converted into an electrical current that can be used to power homes and businesses. This not only helps to reduce our carbon footprint, but also provides an affordable and sustainable energy source for generations to come.

What do I need to know before going solar?

Installing a solar energy system is a significant investment and requires careful consideration. From the reputation of the contractor to the cost and system size, there are many factors to weigh before making a decision.

At IBG Solar, we understand the importance of making an informed decision about solar energy. Our team of experts is here to help guide you through the decision-making process and provide a customized solution that meets your unique needs and goals.

How can I find the best Solar Installer?

Choosing a trustworthy solar installer is a critical step in the process of installing a solar energy system on your home. There are several factors to consider when making this decision, including:

  • Peer recommendations and consumer reviews
  • Seeking multiple quotes and understanding your estimate
  • Professional certifications of the installer
  • The quality of customer service provided
  • Financing options available
  • References from past clients to ensure the installer has a track record of delivering quality work and customer satisfaction.
What is Net Metering and how does it work?

If you have a solar energy system in India, you may be eligible for net metering. Net metering is a policy that allows solar energy system owners to connect their system to the electricity grid and sell excess electricity back to the utility company. When you generate electricity from your solar system, it reduces the amount of energy you need to purchase from the utility, resulting in lower monthly electricity bills. If your solar system produces more energy than you need, the excess power is fed back to the grid, and you receive credits on your electricity bill. These credits can be used to offset future electricity bills or be carried forward to the next billing cycle.

How do changing seasons impact my solar system’s performance?

We are often asked about the various factors that can affect the energy output of a solar array in India. Geography is a crucial factor that influences solar energy generation. India receives abundant sunlight for most parts of the year, making it a prime location for solar energy systems. Other factors that can affect solar energy generation include the cleanliness of solar panels and weather and seasonal variations. The angle of the sun changes throughout the day and year, which can impact the performance of solar panels.

What is the typical Return on Investment for a solar array?

The return on investment for a solar array depends on several factors, including the size and cost of the system, the amount of electricity generated, and the price of electricity in your area. In general, the larger the system and the higher the electricity rates in your area, the greater the return on investment. However, the cost of solar systems has decreased significantly in recent years, making solar energy more accessible and affordable for homeowners and businesses alike. Many solar installers offer financing options and incentives to help reduce the upfront costs of installation, which can help improve the return on investment. It's important to do a thorough cost-benefit analysis and consult with a reputable solar installer to determine the potential return on investment for your specific situation.

What is the “payback” period for a solar system and how long will it last?

The payback period for a solar system varies depending on a number of factors, such as the cost of the system, the amount of energy it produces, and the cost of energy in your area. Generally speaking, most solar systems pay for themselves in 5 to 10 years. After that, you can expect to see a return on your investment for the life of the system, which can range from 20 to 30 years. However, it is important to note that the lifespan of a solar system depends on the quality of the materials and installation, as well as regular maintenance. At IBG Solar, we use only the highest quality materials and provide professional installation and maintenance services to ensure that your solar system will last for many years to come.

What Payment Options are available?

At IBG Solar, we offer a variety of payment options to help make solar energy accessible to everyone. Some of the options include:

  • Cash purchase: This is the most straightforward option where you pay the full cost of the system upfront.
  • Solar loans: We partner with various lenders to offer competitive financing options for those who want to spread the cost of the system over time.
  • Solar leases: With a solar lease, you pay a monthly fee to use the solar system and any excess energy generated is sold back to the grid.
  • Power Purchase Agreements (PPAs): Similar to a solar lease, with a PPA you pay a fixed rate for the electricity generated by the solar system, typically for a set period of time.
  • Our solar experts can work with you to determine which payment option is the best fit for your needs and budget. Contact us today to learn more.
